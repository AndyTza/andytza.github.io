University of Washington Planetarium Coordinator
<div style="border: 2px solid #4CAF50; padding: 10px; border-radius: 10px; margin-bottom: 20px;">
  <h2 style="color: #4CAF50;">About the Planetarium</h2>
  <p>As the new graduate student planetarium coordinator, I'm excited to build on the momentum of my predecessors. Our planetarium, equipped with state-of-the-art projectors and software, offers stunning visual experiences. We present 150 free shows annually to local schools and communities, inspiring countless minds.</p>
